This project was carried out in cooperation with the real Frederick County Visitor Center. I was invited in to take exhaustive measurements and reference photos, and our finished Virtual Reality experience was made available as an online extension of the visitor center during COVID-19 lockdowns. The one-to-one digital replica showcases Frederick County's history and attractions from the comfort of one's own home.

I encourage you to explore this scene in 3D or VR via BraincaveXR using your Meta Quest headset or desktop computer. (Chrome recommended)
